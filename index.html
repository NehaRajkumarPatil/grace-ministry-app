Privacy Policy
Grace Ministry India

Last updated: March 2026

Grace Ministry India is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

Prayer requests (name, message, city), event registrations, and FCM tokens for push notifications.

How We Use It

Your data is used solely for ministry communication to pray for your needs, send event reminders, and notify you of new sermons. We never sell or share your data.

Data Security

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase with encryption. Access is restricted to authorized ministry staff.

Push Notifications

You can disable push notifications at any time via your browser or device settings.
